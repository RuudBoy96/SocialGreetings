Frequently asked questions

Privacy, security, and how Love Messages cards work.

Privacy & security

Is my WhatsApp chat safe when I upload it?

Yes — we treat your upload as private and temporary. Your .txt file is processed on our server to build your card, then deleted immediately. The resulting card data is kept for 30 minutes and then auto-deleted. You can also hit Delete now on your card preview to remove everything right away. Read our full Privacy Policy.

Do you store my messages permanently?

No. Card data is automatically deleted after 30 minutes. We do not keep a permanent archive of your chats.

What happens if I close the browser?

Your card data remains on our server until the 30-minute timer expires or you click Delete now. Closing the browser does not trigger immediate deletion — use the Delete now button if you want to remove your data right away.

Can other people see my card?

Only if they have your unique preview link. There is no public gallery or search. Treat the link like an unlisted URL — share it only with people you trust.

Do you share data with print partners?

No. When you order a print, you download a PDF and upload it yourself to a print service. We do not automatically send your chat or card data to Printed.com, Prodigi, Gelato, or any other partner.

Are my chats sent to AI services?

No. Your chat export is processed locally on our server using our own code — we do not send your messages to OpenAI or other third-party AI services.

Can I delete my data before 30 minutes?

Yes. On your card preview page, click Delete now. Your card data will be removed immediately and the preview link will stop working.

What file types do you accept?

We accept .txt chat export files from WhatsApp, iMessage, and Messenger (without media). We auto-detect which platform your export came from.

Product

How do I export my chat from WhatsApp?

Open the chat → tap the contact name → Export chat → Without media. Save the .txt file and upload it here.

How do I export from iMessage?

On Mac, use an export tool or copy messages to a .txt file. Common formats include "iMessage Chat with [name].txt" — we auto-detect the format.

How do I export from Messenger?

In Messenger, go to your profile → Download your information → select Messages. Upload the resulting .txt export file.

What is a Love Messages card?

A greeting card whose front pages are a collage of every message in your chat about things you love and moments you share — styled like your real messaging app (WhatsApp, iMessage, or Messenger).

Can I edit the inside message after creating?

Yes. On the card preview page, use the inside message editor below the carousel. Your changes update live and are saved when you click Save.
