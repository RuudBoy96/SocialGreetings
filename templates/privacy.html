Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

SocialGreetings helps you turn chat exports into printable greeting cards. We take your privacy seriously — this page explains exactly what we do with your data.

What we collect

  • Chat export file — when you upload a .txt file from WhatsApp, iMessage, or Messenger.
  • Processed card data — the love messages we extract, your chosen names, orientation, and any inside message you write on the preview page.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your chat data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use your messages to train AI models.
  • We do not post your card or messages on social media.
  • We do not show advertising based on your uploads.
  • We do not require you to link your messaging accounts — you simply upload a file you exported yourself.

How long we keep your data

  • Raw upload (.txt): deleted immediately after we process your chat and build your card.
  • Card data: stored for {{ retention_minutes|default(30) }} minutes of inactivity, then automatically deleted. While your card page is open, a heartbeat keeps it alive.
  • Delete now: you can remove your card data immediately from the card preview page at any time.
  • Encrypted at rest: for the short time your card exists, its contents are encrypted on the server.

Closing your browser tab does not instantly delete your card — the inactivity timer and the Delete now button do. See our FAQ for more detail.

Where your data lives

Card data is stored temporarily on the server running SocialGreetings. This data is excluded from our public code repository and is never published to GitHub.

Who can see your card

Your card is accessible to anyone who has your unique preview link (a long, unguessable URL). We recommend not sharing this link publicly if your messages are very personal. There is no public gallery or search — only people with the link can view your card.

Your rights

  • Delete your data anytime using the Delete now button on your card preview.
  • No account is required — you don't need to log in to request deletion.
  • After deletion or expiry, your card data cannot be recovered.

Security

  • When deployed publicly, we use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.
  • We do not store passwords (no accounts in the current version).
  • Processing happens on our server — your chat is not sent to external AI services.
  • Strict security headers and a content security policy protect the site.

Print partners

If you order a print, you download a PDF and upload it to a third-party print service (e.g. Prodigi, Printed.com, Gelato). Those services have their own privacy policies. We do not automatically send your chat data to print partners — only the PDF you choose to upload.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@socialgreetings.com.
