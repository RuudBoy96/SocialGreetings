Privacy & security

Your conversations are deeply personal. We built SocialGreetings so your messages can never be stolen, sold, or seen by anyone but you.

🗑️ Your chat file is deleted instantly

The file you upload is processed in memory to build your card and then deleted the moment your card is generated. We never keep your raw chat export.

⏱️ Cards auto-delete after {{ retention_minutes }} minutes

Your card stays available while you have it open. After {{ retention_minutes }} minutes of inactivity it is permanently deleted — and you can delete it yourself at any time with one click.

🔐 Encrypted while it exists

For the short time your card exists, its contents are encrypted at rest, so the stored data is useless to anyone who should not have it.

🔗 Private, unguessable links

Every card lives behind a long, random link that can't be guessed or enumerated. No account, email, or password required.

🚫 Never sold, shared, or used for ads

Your messages are used for one thing only — making your card. We don't sell or share your data, and there are no third-party advertising trackers.

🛡️ Secure by design

Traffic is served with strict security headers and a content security policy, and your message content is never sent to any outside service.
